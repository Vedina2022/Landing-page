Migiya is a village in Pervomaiskyi Raion, Mykolaiv Oblast, Ukraine. Migiya is a real mountain country among the steppes. International kayak and rafting competitions are held on the Migiya rapids, as the kayak route ranks 2nd in Ukraine in terms of the difficulty of ascent. Migia became the winner of the international interactive competition "Wonders of the Lands of St. Nicholas".
